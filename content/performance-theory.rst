Performance theory
==================


.. objectives::

   - WRITE ME


.. keypoints::

Speedup:

.. math::

   \mathrm{speedup} = \frac{T_{1}}{T_{P}}


Efficiency:

.. math::

   \mathrm{efficiency} = \frac{\mathrm{speedup}}{P} = \frac{T_{1}}{PT_{P}}
